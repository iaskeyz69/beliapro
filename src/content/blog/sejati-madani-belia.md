**We, BELIAPRO, are proud to have attended Program Santuni Pertubuhan Belia Tahun 2026 (SejaTi MADANI x BELIA)** — representing Kelab Belia Progresif Datuk Keramat among youth organizations from across Malaysia.

This was a meaningful step for our club as we continue to engage with national programmes that support youth development, community empowerment, and the values we stand for as a registered youth organization in **Datuk Keramat, Kuala Lumpur**.

## Event Details

| | |
|---|---|
| **Tarikh:** | 14 Mac 2026 (Sabtu) |
| **Tempat:** | Dewan Podium, Menara KBS, Presint 4, WP Putrajaya |
| **Format:** | Secara bersemuka (face-to-face) |

The programme was held physically at **Menara KBS, Putrajaya** — bringing together youth organizations under one roof for a day of engagement, learning, and national-level dialogue on youth community development.

## Our Participation

Members of BELIAPRO were on the ground at **Dewan Podium** to take part in the session, connect with fellow youth leaders, and represent our club's commitment to building youth character, national loyalty, and positive community impact through the principles of **Rukun Negara**.

For us, attending programmes like this is not just about presence — it is about showing that **BELIAPRO is active, registered, and ready to contribute** to Malaysia's youth development agenda.
