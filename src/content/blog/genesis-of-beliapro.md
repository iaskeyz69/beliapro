Kelab Belia Progresif Datuk Keramat, known by its abbreviation **BELIAPRO**, is a Malaysian youth organization based in Kuala Lumpur dedicated to the development and empowerment of young people.

## Our Mission

The primary mission of our club is to build youth character and increase national loyalty through the principles of **Rukun Negara**. We believe that every young Malaysian has the potential to contribute positively to their community and nation.

## What We Do

BELIAPRO runs programmes across multiple areas of youth development:

- Raising awareness regarding negative values and influences
- Promoting youth involvement in culture, environment, sports, health, spirituality, and social welfare
- Community development programmes in urban and rural areas
- Training in leadership, skills, and entrepreneurship
- Education and life skills for positive personal development
