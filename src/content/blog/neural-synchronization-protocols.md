At BELIAPRO, the principles of **Rukun Negara** are not just words on a wall — they are the foundation of everything we do.

## Belief in God

We encourage spiritual awareness and moral integrity among our members, respecting the diverse faiths that make up Malaysia's rich cultural fabric.

## Loyalty to King and Country

National loyalty is central to our mission. We foster a deep love for Malaysia and a commitment to its unity and progress.

## Upholding the Constitution

We teach our members to respect the rule of law and understand their rights and responsibilities as Malaysian citizens.

## Rule of Law

Discipline, accountability, and respect for authority are values we instil through our programmes and activities.

## Good Behaviour and Morality

Building character means promoting positive behaviour, rejecting negative influences, and developing young people who lead by example.
