Leadership is not born — it is built. BELIAPRO's leadership training programmes are designed to develop the next generation of Malaysian leaders.

## What We Offer

Our leadership programmes include:

- **Workshops and seminars** on communication, teamwork, and decision-making
- **Entrepreneurship training** for young people interested in starting their own ventures
- **Mentorship** from experienced community leaders and professionals
- **Practical projects** where members lead real community initiatives

## Skills for Life

Beyond leadership, our training covers life skills that help youth face challenges with confidence — from public speaking to financial literacy to conflict resolution.

Our programmes are open to all young people in the Kuala Lumpur area. [Enquire about upcoming sessions](/contact).
