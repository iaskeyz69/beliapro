Malaysia's cultural diversity is one of our greatest strengths. BELIAPRO's culture and heritage programmes help young people appreciate and celebrate this richness.

## Celebrating Our Heritage

Through cultural events, traditional arts activities, and heritage programmes, we connect youth with the traditions that define who we are as Malaysians.

## Arts & Creative Expression

We support creative expression through arts and crafts programmes, giving young people outlets to explore their talents and express their identity.

## National Identity

Understanding our cultural roots builds a stronger sense of national identity — a key part of BELIAPRO's mission to increase national loyalty among youth.
